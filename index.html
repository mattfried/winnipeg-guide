Winnipeg Manitoba

The best drinks, eats, and everything else at the center of Canada.

Welcome!

Winnipeg continues to be one of the most cultural cities in Canada, in combination with keeping its rich history on full display.

Trendy coffee shops to classy restaurants and you can't forget succulent dessert spots, Winnipeg has it all.

If sports are more your thing, then you're in luck with the local Winnipeg Jets. Spend an evening attending a game if you're fortunate enough to get your hands on a set of tickets!

Food, Coffee, & Treats

Winnipeg has a wide variety of areas within the city that ooze food and drink spots with some unique and some consisting of the usual popular establishments.

Located in the heart of the city, The Forks is a year-round hot spot that does not lack eats and drinks and things to do and see. Other notable locations in the city include but are not limited to Osborne Village, Corydon, St. Boniface, and Fort Garry just to name a few.

Winnipeg Jets

Hockey is Winnipeg's sport and is home to the most passionate fanbase in the entire NHL.

BellMTS Center in downtown Winnipeg is buzzing every NHL game night when the local Winnipeg Jets are hosting. Attending a Winnipeg Jets game at BellMTS Center with 15,000+ other passionate fans is a must if you have the opportunity.
